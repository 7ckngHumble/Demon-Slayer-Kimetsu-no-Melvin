Tanjiro Kamado

A kindhearted boy. He lived a blissful existence with his mother and siblings, but were attacked by demons while he was out working. He joins the Demon Slayer Corps in order to turn his sister Nezuko, who has become a demon, back into a human, as well as to avenge the death of his family by hunting down the demon who killed them. Under the tutelage of Sakonji Urokodaki, he learns to wield "Water Breathing," so that he can face the demon threat.

But during the battle at Mount Natagumo, he remembered the memory of his late father's kagura dance, and applied that movement to learn a new Breathing called "Hinokami Kagura" also known as "The Sun Breathing", the first breathing form.

Nezuko Kamado

Tanjiro's younger sister, who's been turned into a demon. Despite being a demon, she never attacks humans, thanks to both Urokodaki's hypnotic suggestion, and her own willpower. She joins Tanjiro in his fight against the demons using her own demonic powers.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

He joins the Demon Slayer Corps at the same time as Tanjiro and Inosuke. He has an acute sense of hearing, and can identify the sounds made by other people or demons. In battle, when he is scared to death, he falls asleep and his personality changes to the swordsman that can unleash his "Thunder Breathing".

Inosuke Hashibira

Highlights

Gyomei Himejima

Gyomei Himejima, also known as the Stone Hashira, Gyomei specializes in strength and endurance, holding the title of the strongest Hashira in the Demon Slayer Corps. During combats, Gyomei relies on brute strength and the weight of his weapons. His unique fighting style, coupled with his ability to sense the presence and movements of enemies, makes him a formidable adversary.

Gyomei's personality is marked by deep emotions and empathy. He is often seen praying for the souls of the demons he slays, acknowledging their tragic existences. Despite past tragedies, including a demon attack on a temple he cared for, Gyomei remains unwavering in his mission to protect humanity.
